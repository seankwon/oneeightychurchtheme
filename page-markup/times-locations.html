Times & Locations

Come to Sunday service! That's where the sermons happen, and then we eat afterwards.

Time: Service starts at 12 noon every Sunday.

Location: Our service is in a movie theater by Union Square:
AMC Loews Theater
890 Broadway (@ 19th St)
New York, NY 10003

How to get there:
Take the 4, 5, 6, N, R, or Q to Union Square, then walk up Broadway to 19th street.
Or if you prefer to drive, there is free street parking in the area on Sundays!

If you're looking for more (say, around the middle of the week) or you just want to go deeper into the message, small groups can help with that.
